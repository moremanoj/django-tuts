Highly experienced and qualified Vehicle Technicians

The Ger’s Garage has emerged with solution of all garage services which is available 24X7 just a click away. The system includes regular car service, major car repair, washing & detailing or any kind of emergency car repair also all kind of small to medium vehicles (i.e. motorbikes, cars, small vans and small buses) The Ger’s Garage aims at making vehicles service and repair hassle free and time efficient along with the facility of pick and drop to make our customers’ life easy. We provide service to vehicle owners and service providers through our tech-enabled website. It’s a ONE STOP solution for all the vehicle owners who have at some point experienced the anguish because of the lack of transparency and up selling by your vehicle service provider. The idea here is to make the whole experience of owning a vehicle more beautiful. We believe customers deserve a first class service – so whatever you need we are only too happy to help.
